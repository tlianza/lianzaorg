--- title: "Get Exceptional" date: 2009-06-01 featured_image: "/images/exceptional_mail.png" technologies: ["C#"] --- Moving from Ruby to C# left me missing some of the great tools the Ruby ecosystem had relied on, including hosted error tracking.
