--- title: "Pivotal Tracker Reports" date: 2011-06-01 technologies: ["Ruby", "Sinatra"] timeline_badge_color: "primary" fontawesome_icon: "fa-list" project_url: "https://github.com/wishpot/pivotal-tracker-reports" resources: - name: featured src: "images/pivotal_tracker_reports.png" --- I've really enjoyed Pivotal Tracker as a development tool, but generating reports for businesspeople to see high-level status was not possible. I built this tool using Sinatra and the Pivotal Tracker API to generate reports that could be emailed out on a weekly basis. They can group by label (to see where we're spending the most resources) and highlight in red or green if we're resolving ore stories than we're creating over a given time period.
